About Sailor Moon

Sailor Moon is a beloved Japanese anime and manga series created by Naoko Takeuchi. It follows the story of Usagi Tsukino, a clumsy yet kind-hearted 14-year-old girl, who transforms into the magical warrior Sailor Moon to protect the world from evil forces. With the help of her friends, the Sailor Guardians (also called Sailor Senshi), and her destined love, Tuxedo Mask, Usagi embarks on a journey of self-discovery, friendship, and love.
